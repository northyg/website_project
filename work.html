Odin's Goes To Work!

Odin says there are many benefits to bringing a dog to work! Odin is always happy to come to work and greet his human co-workers. He excels at bringing a smile to their face. He loves people no matter what (except the delivery people, he was wary of them at first, but we worked on that.)

Odin is a Beaver fan of course, but his U of O co-workers love him even though he sports OSU gear!

His favorite activity is to say hi to everyone in the morning and do tricks for treats!
