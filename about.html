About Odin!

Odin and his two sisters were originally found as strays in Northern California and brought to an Oregon Humane society when they were ~14 weeks old. No one knows what breeds they are, but many people guessed part pitbull because of the brindle coat.

As Odin got older, he grew tall and lean and weighs 70lbs. Now people guess that he's part Shepherd / Great Dane.

Either way, Odin has grown to be an amazing dog who loves to learn new tricks, loves his family and an all around great dog.

Special thanks to Tab Shamsi of Training Positive for the incredibly helpful Youtube video's he published on positive dog training techniques.
